What is the idea behind How Are You?
How Are You was created to help you to get to know yourself better. By measuring your mood and main causes of it, after some space in time you are able to conclude what makes you down, and up! Moreover, which places, days of week or months are more favorable for you and which ones are not the very best for you. Knowing yourself better makes you more self-aware, emotionally stable and peaceful.
How scientificaly proven is How Are You?
We put a lot of efforts to be reliable. To determine your mood we use scientifically well proven test called I-PANAS-SF. Additionally, our calculations and conclusions are validated by qualified psychologist.
Where can I get more informations about the topic?
We are deeply related into Quantified Self movement which concerns measuring every aspect of life. You can find a lot of videos and talks about it on the official Quantified Self website. If you are interested in mood tracking process you can also see PANAS test research paper.
