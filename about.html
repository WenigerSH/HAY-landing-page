What is the idea behind How Are You?
How Are You is here to help you know yourself better. By measuring your mood and circumastances of it you can (after a period of time) tell what makes you down, and up. What places, day of week or months are better for you or with ones you should avoid. Knowing yourself makes you more self-aware, stable and peacefull. On the other hand tracking your mood gives you bit more control of it over time thus improving your overall mood.
How scientificaly proven is How Are You?
We put a lot of effort to be reliable. To determine your mood we use scientificaly well proven test called I-PANAS-SF and our calculations and conclusions are validated by qualified psychilogist.
Where can I get more informations about the topic?
We are deeply into Quantified Self movement with is about measuring every aspect of life. You can find a lot of video information and talks about it on offical Quantified Self page. If your intrested in mood tracking you can also see PANAS test resarch paper.
What about privacy and data safety?
All gathered data are fully anonymous, we don't store personal data, like name or e-mail, along with your moods. We can;t connect given person with he's data so there is no risk of privacy leak.
